Bookmarker

Mark your place in the dashboard to go back to later.

Bookmarker adds a small bookmark button to posts. Click on one to mark that post in your dashboard. Jump back to it at any time by clicking on the bookmark entry in your Tumblr sidebar.

Goto Dash Post

Jump directly from a post permalink page to its position on the dashboard.

Goto Dash Post adds a "↵ Dash" button to the control buttons at the top-right of a post permalink page of a Tumblr you follow. Click on it to jump to that post's position in your dashboard.

Magnifier

Enlarge photos without leaving the dashboard.

Magnifier adds a small icon to photo and photoset posts on the dashboard. Click on it to view the full-size images without leaving the dash.

Mass Editor Tweaks

Select multiple posts in the Tumblr Mass Editor.

Mass Editor Tweaks adds a Select menu to the Tumblr Mass Editor that allows you to select posts in groups of 100, or by post type (including answered asks and private posts).

Reblog Yourself

Improve reblogging functionality to your own Tumblr posts.

Reblog Yourself allows you to reblog a post to any of the blogs associated with your account, including the same one that originally posted it.

Reply Replies

Automatically create reply posts for responding to post comments and other notes.

With Reply Replies, when you click on the icon of a notification on your dash (or in a dashboard post when you click the notes count button), it will open a new post with that notification in it, ready for you to comment on and publish.

Safe Dash

In public? Hide images in Tumblr posts until you hover your mouse over them (in case they're unsafe).

Sometimes your Tumblr dashboard can be a racy place. Safe Dash lets you hide all images in your dashboard behind a grey box. Only when you point your mouse at these boxes will the images appear.

No more worrying about what might appear on your dashboard when you are at work or near young children!

Timestamps

Add the date and time to a post, message or submission.

The Timestamps feature adds a customizable date and time stamp to posts in your dashboard, answered and unanswered messages (not Fan Mail) in your inbox, as well as unpublished submissions.
