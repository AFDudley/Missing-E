Always getting better.

Missing e is constantly improving! When there are changes to the Tumblr interface or when a great new feature idea comes up, you'll have the extension that is first to improve your Tumbling! Stay Updated on Tumblr

Completely customizable.

Whether you want to reply to replies, see all of a post's tags or reblog without leaving the Tumblr dashboard, Missing e has what you need! Something you don't want? Easy. You can disable any feature and customize all sorts of options!

Simpler and faster.

Some extensions for Tumblr can take forever to load. Others require you to install all sorts of extra stuff before you can get it working the way you want. Do away with trouble like that by installing Missing e.
